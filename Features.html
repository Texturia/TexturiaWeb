Features

Enchance Your Game with Texturia

Provide a real life sample of the material and Texturia can create a perfect replicate in the virtual world.

With the use of Texturia’s special camera and sensors, Texturia is able to pick up real life textures, colours, designs, depth and even reflectivity of the sample and replicate it in the virtual world.



If you can’t provide a sample, have your game developers work closely with our talented design team to recreate the desired look.



Select one of the existing textures/designs in Texturia’s database.

Texturia has hundreds of textures ready to be used.



Work with Texturia’s design team to edit textures and designs.

Any texture/design can be edited to meet your standards. This can include color changes, adding designs and patterns, editing depth, etc.



Gain access to Texturia’s software that allows for optimal rendering, creating smooth-running games.
