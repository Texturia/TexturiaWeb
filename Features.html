Request existing or custom textures and designs to be applied to virtual furniture, objects, scenery, clothing and more!

Select one of the existing textures/designs we have in our database, or request a new one. If your desired object can’t be found in our database, or if you’d like something more specific, send us a real life sample of the texture/design you want to replicate and Texturia will use a camera with special sensors that is able to pick the texture and colour of the design in question. If you are not able to provide a sample of the desired design, it's not a problem! Your developers can work closely with our design team to describe their desired look.



We will edit the textures and designs using our advanced software

Work with our design team to edit textures and designs to meet your standards and create hyper realistic games. This can include color changes, adding designs and patterns, editing depth, etc.



Enhance your game by adding our designs to your virtual space

The hyper realistic textures and designs that have been electronically reproduced can be applied to the virtual world, giving users the chance to experience a new reality. Our software allows these to be smoothly applied to 3-D games (Virtual Reality) or regular 2-D video games.
