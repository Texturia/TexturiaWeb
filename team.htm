INFO

Texturia allows for hyper realistic textures to be applied to the virtual world (ei: virtual reality, video games, etc). Our technology captures real life textures, colours and designs, electronically reproduces them and allows you to apply them virtually (as skins, objects,scenery, etc.) We aim to partner with Virtual Reality companies (Oculus, HTC Vive, Google, Microsoft, etc) as well as video game manufacturers to sell these products within the space.
