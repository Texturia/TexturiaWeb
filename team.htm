INFO

Texturia allows for hyper realistic textures to be applied to the virtual world (ie: virtual reality, video games, etc). Our technology captures real life textures, colours and designs, electronically reproduces them and allows you to apply them virtually (as skins, objects,scenery, etc.) We aim to sell these virtual products to video game manufacturers.
