Texturia is an application that allows it's users to significantly increase the fidelity of virtual textures through the capture of real life elements, with the simple click of a button

Use of Texturia in games and Personal Projects

Texturia's array of different life-like digital textures are of open use for any individual personal projects or small group projects.

The use of Texturia assets in products sold commercially are subject to the standardized fee of $5 per Megabyte

For corporate partners/users, using Texturia's end of the line Textures aren't the only benefits that can be reaped
